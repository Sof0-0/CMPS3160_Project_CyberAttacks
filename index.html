Who Do Threat Actors Target in the Cyberspace? What Are They Looking For?¶

GitHub.io page: https://sof0-0.github.io

In this project, I am planning to use CISSM Cyber Events Dataset.

Dataset and Project Description:

The information in the dataset brings together open-source information surrounding a range of publically acknowledged cyber events on private and public organizations.
Events from 2014 through present have been coded to standardize information on threat actor, threat actor country, motive, target, end effects, industry, and country of impact.

Research Questions:

Furthermore, I am planning to address several questions in this project: Based on the data from the first database, we can build the classifier model to categorize each threat actor based on certain characteristics:

  1. Are there any characteristics that we found in the FullThreatActors that allow us to classify various threat actors?
  2. Can we perform the opposite operation by classifying the certain actor type based on the target country and the threat actor name?

Based on the data from the second database, we can also build a multi-class classifier and determine the motive of the attackers based on the victim's industry, country, and the attack type.

  1. What are the most essential characteristics defining the motive of the attack?
  2. Can we make a claim that only certain types of motives are present per industry or per country?
  3. Is there a direct correlation between victim's characteristics and the attacker's motive or the type of the attack?

Model Planning:

Having this questions as our main objectives, I am planning to build either the KNN classifier or the logistic regression model and experiment with various features, further performing the cross validation to get the best prediction score on the new data.

I also would like to compare various models types (log regression with KNN Classifier) and determine if one of them performs better on the cross-variable predictions (identifying trends between the attack Types and the Sub-Type from the same dataset) vs the cross-sets predictions (predicting the Victim based on the Attacker's characteristics and motive or the country location; or, determining the Attacker type based on the victim's main characteristics).

From the FullThreatActors.csv:

  • Use the actor type, country to predict the Threat Actor Name (if there is a correlation).
  • ^ (reverse): Use the name, country to predict the Actor Type.

From the FullAttackList.csv:

  • Use victim's location, industry to predict the motive (if there is a correlation).
  • Use the victim's location, industry, and actor location to predict the motive (does the actor location improves the prediction?)
  • Having the actor location, type and the motive, can we predict the country and the industry of the victim for the next attack?

I believe that for the last objective on the modeling, the KNN classifier will be more feasible as the data is not linearly separable (would have to do the polynomial fit or introduce the penalty for the logistic regressor).
