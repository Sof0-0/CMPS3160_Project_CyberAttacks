In this project, I am planning to use CISSM Cyber Events Dataset.
The information in the dataset brings together open-source information surrounding a range of publically acknowledged cyber events on private and public organizations.
Events from 2014 through present have been coded to standardize information on threat actor, threat actor country, motive, target, end effects, industry, and country of impact.
